# Agile Maturity Scoring Worksheet

Purpose:
Provide a structured scoring mechanism to accompany the Agile Maturity Model.

This worksheet supports:

- Quarterly team assessments
- Program-level capability reviews
- Transformation roadmap alignment
- Coaching backlog prioritization

Scoring scale:

1 = Ad Hoc  
2 = Structured  
3 = Predictable  
4 = Optimized  
5 = Adaptive  

Score based on observable behavior and metrics, not opinion.

# 7. Interpretation Guide

2.0 to 2.9 = Structured

Do not chase a level.
