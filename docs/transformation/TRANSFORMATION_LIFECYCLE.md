# Transformation Lifecycle

Agile transformation is not a one-time rollout. It operates as a continuous improvement cycle that connects strategy, capability development, delivery performance, and leadership review.

This lifecycle ensures that transformation efforts remain aligned with business outcomes and organizational learning.

## 7. Executive Review

Leadership reviews transformation progress and adjusts priorities.

Typical cadence:

- quarterly transformation reviews
- roadmap updates
- backlog reprioritization

See:

- [Quarterly Executive Review Template](QUARTERLY_EXECUTIVE_REVIEW_TEMPLATE.md)

## Continuous Learning Loop

Insights from executive reviews feed back into the transformation roadmap, creating a continuous improvement loop that evolves the Agile operating model over time.
