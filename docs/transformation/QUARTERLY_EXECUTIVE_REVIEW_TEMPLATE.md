# Quarterly Executive Transformation Review

Audience:
- CIO
- CTO
- VP Engineering
- Product leadership
- Transformation sponsors

Purpose:
Provide structured review of transformation health, systemic impediments, and strategic alignment.

Timebox: 90 minutes

---

# 1. Executive Summary (10 minutes)

Provide a concise overview:

- Overall maturity trend
- Delivery stability trend
- Major systemic risks
- Major wins

Score snapshot:

- Overall maturity score:
- Delivery trend: Improving / Stable / Declining
- Quality trend: Improving / Stable / Declining
- Adoption trend: Improving / Stable / Declining
- Cultural signal: Improving / Stable / Declining

---

# 2. Flow and Delivery Review (20 minutes)

Review:

- Lead time trend (quarter over quarter)
- Throughput stability
- Commitment reliability
- Blocked work percentage

Discussion prompts:

- Where is variability increasing?
- Where is WIP accumulating?
- Are cross-team dependencies slowing flow?

Decision outputs:

- Structural changes required
- Resource adjustments
- Dependency ownership clarification

---

# 3. Product and Outcome Review (15 minutes)

Review:

- OKR progress
- Feature adoption data
- Time from release to feedback

Discussion prompts:

- Are we shipping validated value?
- Where are we delivering output without outcome?
- Are we funding discovery sufficiently?

Decision outputs:

- Portfolio reprioritization
- Discovery investment changes
- Outcome measurement improvements

---

# 4. Quality and Technical Health (15 minutes)

Review:

- Defect escape rate
- Rework rate
- Deployment frequency
- Technical debt indicators

Discussion prompts:

- Is quality debt accumulating?
- Are deployment constraints slowing innovation?
- Are we under-investing in engineering excellence?

Decision outputs:

- Investment adjustments
- Quality threshold changes
- Technical modernization priorities

---

# 5. Cultural and Governance Health (15 minutes)

Review:

- Psychological safety trend
- Retro action completion rate
- Escalation frequency
- Escalation resolution latency

Discussion prompts:

- Are teams surfacing risk early?
- Are leaders removing impediments quickly?
- Is governance slowing flow?

Decision outputs:

- Incentive alignment changes
- Leadership behavioral adjustments
- Escalation model refinements

---

# 6. Maturity Review (10 minutes)

Review maturity scoring worksheet:

- Lowest scoring dimension
- Most improved dimension
- Systemic constraint limiting progress

Decision outputs:

- Next quarter transformation focus
- Coaching allocation
- Governance adjustments

---

# 7. Executive Commitments (5 minutes)

Each executive states:

- One structural change they will sponsor
- One behavior they will model
- One systemic impediment they will remove

Document commitments publicly.

---

# 8. Review Outputs

Record:

- Approved changes
- Structural adjustments
- Investment shifts
- Risk mitigation plans
- Coaching focus updates

Publish summary within one week.

Transformation progress must be visible at the executive level.
